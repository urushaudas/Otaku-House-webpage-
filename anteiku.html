About Anteiku Coffee Shop

The Anteiku Coffee shop is inspired by the famous anime Tokyo Ghoul. We serve normal coffees with a little touch of the anime to link it with the main theme of the anime. People can also come here to have a chill time while reading books or novels or doing their own work.

The first anime themed house for multiple fun and entertainment all in one house.
