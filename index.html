About Us

Otaku house is the first anime themed mini house which was established by EatVenture Nepal. The prime idea of this house was of Mr. Hari Ghimire. This house containes tons of attractions specially designed for anime lovers all over Nepal. Inside the house, there is a restaurant, a coffee shop, a bakery shop, anime store, a photo studio and a karaoke room. With these many kinds of attractions, every anime lover would want to come to the Otaku House.

Services

Otaku Restaurant

This restaurant is an anime themed restaurant serving Japanese foods to replicate the food from popular anime.

Anteiku Coffee Shop

This coffe shop is inspired by the famous anime Tokyo Ghoul.

Anime Stores

The Otaku House also has anime stores where you can get a different variety of anime goods.

Karaoke Rooms

The Otaku House also has arrangement of karaoke rooms for the customers.

Photo Studio

The Otaku House also has a photo studio that consists of a mini exhibition hall and a place where the customers can click pictures with the pictures of famous anime characters.

Bakery Shop

The Otaku House also consists of a bakery shop which is also inspired by anime characters.
