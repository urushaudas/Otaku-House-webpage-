About Otaku Restaurant

The Otaku restaurant is an anime themed restaurant which serves original Japanese cuisine prepared by the gourmet chefs to replicate the food from popular animes. The interiors of the restaurant is inspired by the anime. The walls are decorated with anime arts and many more. The dishes are also served by the cosplayers inspired by popular animes of present time. The restaurant also has the facility of reserving a table amd whole hall for special events. The restaurant also offers takeaway facilities.

The first anime themed house for multiple fun and entertainment all in one house.
