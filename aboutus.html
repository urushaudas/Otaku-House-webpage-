About Otaku House

Otaku house is the first anime themed mini house which was established by EatVenture Nepal. The prime idea of this house was of Mr. Hari Ghimire. This house containes tons of attractions specially designed for anime lovers all over Nepal. The house contains an anime themed restaurant called Otaku restaurant, Anteiku coffee shop, an anime store, karaoke rooms, a photo studio and a mini bakery shop. The soul puropse of the Otaku House is to entertain every customer to the fullest.
